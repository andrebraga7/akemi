A little bit about us and some of our projects

About us

Akemi Renewable Energies was stablished in 2019 and delivers solutions for all types of renewable energy demands. From solar, to wind to hydropower, we're able to provide a full cicle approach. From consulting to final setup we're there along the hole way.


With our headquarters locate in Alto Taquari in the state of Mato Grosso, Brazil, we currently serve the whole of Brazil. From small residential projects, to large commercial aplications and agrobusiness. No matter the demands we have a solution to offer.

Some of our projects

Parking roof

Developed together with Alto Taquari Shopping, this project integrates solar panels as roof for some parking cover. A great example of how renewable energy is seamlessly integrated into day to day aplications, providing efficency and cost savings.

Alto Solar Farm One

Developed in 2021, this state of the art solar farm located in Alto Taquari - MT, Brazil has an installed capacity of 1MW. It's a pilot project that demonstrates the efficency and return on investiment for solar energy.

Residential building

This residential building has it's entire roof area covered in solar panels, providing 40% of all the buildings energy demands throughout the year. It provides costs reduduction for the general building management fees and bills.
